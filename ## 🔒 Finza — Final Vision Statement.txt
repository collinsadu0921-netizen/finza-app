# Finza Vision (Locked)

### **Finza is business software that produces accounting automatically.**

Accounting is **not a task** performed by the business.
Accounting is a **byproduct of real business activity**.

Every sale, invoice, payment, expense, refund, stock movement, and adjustment creates a **verifiable financial record** at the moment it happens.

No reconstruction.
No retroactive cleanup.
No “send me your statements.”

---

## Core Principle

> **If it happened in the business, it must already exist in the books.**

Finza enforces this by design, not by training.

---

## The Three Workspaces (Non-Negotiable)

### 1️⃣ Retail Workspace — **Operate the business**

* Built for shops, cashiers, and managers
* Uses operational language only (sell, refund, stock, cash)
* **No accounting knowledge required**
* No debits, credits, journals, or periods exposed
* All financial truth is created automatically via immutable ledger posting
* Ledger is **write-only via system rules**
* Accounting workspace is **inaccessible**

**Retail exists to run the business — not to explain accounting.**

---

### 2️⃣ Service Workspace — **Run the business and see the truth**

* Built for service businesses, founders, and in-house bookkeepers
* Business language first (invoices, payments, expenses)
* Accounting visibility is **optional**, not hidden
* Users may view:

  * Profit & Loss
  * Balance Sheet
  * General Ledger
* Users may **not**:

  * Post manual journals
  * Close periods
  * Perform accounting adjustments
* Accounting is automatic, but **transparency is allowed**

**Service respects reality: some businesses want insight without being accountants.**

---

### 3️⃣ Accounting-First Workspace — **Professional authority**

* Built exclusively for accounting firms and professionals
* The **only place** where accounting decisions are made:

  * Period control
  * Adjustments
  * Reconciliation
  * Closing
  * Audit readiness
* Manages both:

  * Finza businesses
  * External clients (via opening balances and imports)
* Operates on the same immutable ledger — no duplication

**This workspace governs truth. Others consume it.**

---

## Accountant Reality (Explicitly Supported)

Finza **does not assume** accountants use Finza.

Three valid models are supported:

1. **No accountant**

   * Business runs independently
   * Books are always correct and audit-ready

2. **External accountant (not on Finza)**

   * Business exports period-accurate books
   * Accountant works in their own system
   * Finza remains source of truth

3. **Finza accounting firm**

   * Firm collaborates directly in Accounting-First workspace
   * Works across many clients
   * Applies professional judgment on immutable data

**All three are first-class. None are forced.**

---

## Ledger Doctrine (Immutable)

* One ledger per business
* Append-only
* No edits or deletions of posted entries
* Corrections occur via new entries
* Every number is traceable to a real business event

**The ledger is law.**

---

## Leakage Elimination (Why Finza Exists)

Finza reduces financial leakage by:

* Capturing truth at the moment of action
* Eliminating after-the-fact bookkeeping
* Enforcing supervision on exceptions
* Locking periods
* Creating audit trails by default

This is **structural prevention**, not process compliance.
